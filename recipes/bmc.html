Baked Mac and Cheese

Tired of regular old Kraft Dinner? Probably not (because it's arguably perfect on its own), but if you're looking to mix it up, here's an excellent recipe to satisfy your mac and cheese cravings with a twist!

Ingredients

  • 1.5lb elbow macaroni
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 3 tablespoons flour
  • 1 tablespoon powdered mustard
  • 3 cups milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 package shredded sharp cheddar (~450g)
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper

Topping

  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup panko bread crumbs
