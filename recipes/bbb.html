Bob's Beautiful Brownies

Sudden craving for brownies? Chocolatey desires overriding all discipline and restraint? Don't worry, to make these low cal brownies all you need is a bowl, a whisk, and a few simple ingredients. These brownies are also freezer friendly! So, if you are looking to not feel too guilty, you can simply freeze any leftovers for later!

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted and HOT
  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • 1 1/8 cup white granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 teaspoons pure vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup all purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Lightly grease an 8-inch square baking pan with cooking oil spray. Line with parchment paper; set aside
  3. Combine hot melted butter, oil and sugar together in a medium-sized bowl. Whisk well for about a minute. Add the eggs and vanilla; beat until lighter in colour
  4. Sift in flour, cocoa powder and salt. Gently fold the dry ingredients into the wet ingredients until comfortably combined (be careful not to over beat)
  5. Pour batter into prepared pan, smoothing the top out evenly (if you're really feeling it, now's the time to top with chocolate chunks/chips)
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the centre of the brownies in the pan no longer jiggles (the brownies will keep baking in the hot pan out of the oven). If testing with a toothpick, the toothpick should come out dirty for fudge-textured brownies
  7. Remove and allow to cool to room temperature before slicing into desired sized brownies

Enjoy!
