Definitely a recipe for normal Chili

The beans! The spices! The "meat"! When it comes to homemade Chili, what's not to love? With a myriad of flavorful ingredients and some simple steps, it's no wonder this is known as South Park's best chili recipe. This homemade recipe pairs perfectly on a crisp fall day with some Radiohead playing in the background. So do what feels right and warm up after a little Sunday sarcastiball, or cozy up to a weekday meal your family will love being a part of!

Ingredients

  • 1.5lb ground 'meat' of your choosing (leaner the better)
  • 1 cup chopped onions
  • 2 cloves of garlic, finely chopped/smashed/bashed/annihilated
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano leaves
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 can black beans (540ml)
  • 1 can red kidney beans (540ml)
