Totally Normal Chili

The beans! The spices! The "meat"! When it comes to homemade Chili, what's not to love? With a myriad of flavorful ingredients and some simple steps, it's no wonder this is known as South Park's best chili recipe. This homemade recipe pairs perfectly on a crisp fall day with some Radiohead playing in the background. So do what feels right and warm up after a little Sunday sarcastiball, or cozy up to a weekday meal your family will love being a part of!

Ingredients

  • 1.5lb ground 'meat' of your choosing (leaner the better)
  • 1 cup chopped onions
  • 2 cloves of garlic, finely chopped/smashed/bashed/obliterated
  • 2 tablespoons chili powder
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano leaves
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon salt/flavor enhancer
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • 1 can diced tomatoes (~420ml)
  • 1 can black beans (540ml)
  • 1 can red kidney beans (540ml)

Steps

  1. In a large pot, cook chosen meat over medium heat for ~15 minutes, stir occasionally until meat is cooked (drain fat if necessary)
  2. Add chopped onions and obliterated garlic, mix then let sit for 5-10 minutes
  3. Add dry ingredients, mix thoroughly
  4. Add diced tomatoes and gently increase heat to bring to a boil
  5. Once boiling, reduce heat slightly below medium and cover. Cook for 1 hour, stirring occasionally
  6. Drain and rinse both cans of beans and add, increase heat slightly above medium, cook uncovered for another 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally or until desired thickness is achieved

Enjoy!
